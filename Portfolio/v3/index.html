About Me

I'm a cybersecurity professional based in Kathmandu, Nepal, passionate about threat intelligence, SOC operations, and building tools that make defenders faster and smarter than attackers.

My work lives at the intersection of offensive security knowledge and defensive strategy — I believe you can't defend what you don't understand. I actively participate in CTF competitions, contribute to open-source security tooling, and write about my findings.

When I'm not hunting threats or writing YARA rules, I'm probably dissecting malware samples, tinkering with homelab infrastructure, or deep in a HackTheBox machine.
